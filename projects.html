Enterprise Case Studies

This page highlights selected professional engagements from regulated and enterprise environments. These examples focus on decision support, risk-aware execution, and structured delivery rather than experimental or startup-style projects. All engagements were executed within formal governance, procurement, and compliance frameworks.

HSM Implementation – Government-Owned Bank

Public Sector / BFSI · Cryptographic Security

Context

A government-owned financial institution required the implementation of a Hardware Security Module (HSM) to meet regulatory cryptographic requirements and improve key management assurance.

My Role
  • Supported requirement analysis and solution alignment with regulatory expectations
  • Coordinated between vendor teams, client stakeholders, and internal delivery units
  • Ensured documentation accuracy for approval and audit readiness
Outcome
  • Improved cryptographic governance and compliance posture
  • Reduced implementation risk through structured coordination
  • Enabled predictable delivery within a regulated environment

Anti-DDoS Solution Evaluation – Tier-1 Commercial Bank

Financial Services · Availability & Network Security

Context

A large commercial bank required enhanced protection against volumetric and application-layer denial-of-service attacks to ensure service continuity and regulatory uptime expectations.

My Role
  • Translated technical DDoS risks into operational and business impact language
  • Supported solution comparison and procurement documentation
  • Facilitated stakeholder understanding across technical and non-technical teams
Outcome
  • Improved decision clarity for senior stakeholders
  • Strengthened service availability planning
  • Aligned security controls with long-term operational continuity
