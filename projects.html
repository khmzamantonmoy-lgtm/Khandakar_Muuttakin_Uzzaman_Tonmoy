HSM Implementation for Government-Owned Commercial Bank

Industry: Government / BFSI · Scope: Cryptographic Security (HSM)

Context

A government-owned commercial bank required the procurement and implementation of Hardware Security Modules (HSM) to strengthen cryptographic controls and meet regulatory and operational security requirements under formal public procurement.

Challenge

The engagement required strict compliance with e-GP procurement processes, coordination across multiple stakeholders, and alignment between technical validation, UAT, and acceptance documentation.

My Role

  • Coordinated between client stakeholders, vendors, and internal technical teams
  • Supported proposal submission and procurement formalities
  • Assisted in UAT, commissioning, and delivery documentation
  • Maintained engagement continuity through billing and closure stages

Outcome

  • Enabled compliant procurement approval
  • Supported secure cryptographic operations
  • Reduced execution risk in a regulated environment
Skills demonstrated: Stakeholder coordination, procurement alignment, risk framing, delivery continuity, technical–commercial communication
